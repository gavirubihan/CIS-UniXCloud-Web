About CIS UniXCloud

Welcome to CIS UniXCloud, your one-stop platform for all your academic resources! We are a team of passionate undergraduate students from the Computing and Information Systems (CIS) department at Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka. Our mission is to simplify your learning experience by providing a seamless way to access and manage your notes, lecture slides, and recorded lectures.

Our Our Vision

At CIS UniXCloud, our goal is to help students like us stay organized and focused on learning. We understand the importance of having quick access to study materials, so we built this platform to make it easier for you to find everything you need in one place.

Our Mission

We aim to create a digital space where students can manage their academic life efficiently, leading to better productivity and success.

Why Choose Us?

  • Access lecture materials anywhere, anytime
  • Offline access to PDFs for seamless studying
  • Real-time notifications for updated resources
  • Easy sharing of academic materials
  • User-friendly interface designed for students
