CIS UniXCloud

CIS UniXCloud is an educational platform for downloading and viewing lecture slides, notes, and recordings. We are now developing a website to offer the same convenience online, making it easy for students to access study materials from any device.

Download App Now

Our Features

Download PDF

Easily download lecture slides, notes in PDF format for offline access

View PDF

View your PDF documents directly within the app, with smooth and reliable performance

Cache System

Store frequently accessed files locally for faster access, even with limited internet connectivity

Notification System

Get instant alerts for new lecture slides, notes, and recordings to stay updated

Share PDF

Easily share lecture slides and notes in PDF format with others directly from the app.

Time Table

Access your Lecture schedule and upcoming events in one place, helping you stay organized and on track.
